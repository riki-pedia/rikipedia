Las Vegas

Las Vegas is a large city in Nevada. It is commonly known for many things, including The Strip, MGM, Casinos, and the Hoover Dam. On July 1, 2023, I went to Vegas, where I saw many things. I wrote about them in my blog. These things include New York-New York, Paris, Circus Circus, and many more.

Casinos

Casinos are very common in Vegas. They are places where people go to gamble their money and hope to win a lot more than they lost. In Vegas, casinos are located in or near The Strip. Usually, casinos have kid-friendly areas, such as arcades. You will find that a majority of casinos in Vegas are owned by MGM. For instance, during my stay in Vegas, we went to a dinner show at a casino/hotel called Excallibur. The show went with the theme, it was called Tournament of Kings.

MGM

In Vegas, MGM owns a lot of the hotel/casino buildings. MGM is a film company that made shows Tom & Jerry, Pink Panther, and many more. Owning many properties and TV franchises may be one thing, but owning luxury hotels is another. When I was in Vegas, I stayed at The Signature at MGM Grand, where they had luxury everything. They had valet, (time-consuming) bell service and much more. They would do anything for you (so long as you tip them).
