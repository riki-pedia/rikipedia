Ranches

Ranches are farms but for animals instead of plants

A ranch is a place used for raising animals such as horses, cattle, chickens, and many more. On a ranch, you could find a farm, but they are not the most likely thing. Ranchers can make a lot of money off of the animals, but you would also have to pay for a ranch. For instance, you could sell chicken eggs or cow milk.
Just ignore the smell x_x.
